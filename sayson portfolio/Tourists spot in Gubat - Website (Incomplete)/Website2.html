Lola Sayong Eco Surf-Camp


Lola Sayong is a an internationally-known tourist destination and eco-surfcamp located at Buenavista, Gubat, Sorsogon, Bicol, Philippines. It is a camp fueled by people that embraces the culture of responsible surfers, ideas of environmentalists and attitudes of pure Gubatnon. Lola Sayong offers an experience the best of Gubat as well as its neighboring towns own.
