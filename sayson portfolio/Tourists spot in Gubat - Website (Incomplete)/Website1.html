Rizal Beach


Rizal Beach in Gubat is a veritable tropical paradise. Known as a bathing and picnic spot among the locals,
the beach's white sand coast is matched by equally clean, clear, azure seawater, making this particular place a favorite among vacationers.
The clean and vast surroundings make it an ideal venue for many exciting beach/water sports and activities.
Visitors have never forgotten the great time they had at Rizal Beach. Rizal Beach is located in the municipality of Gubat, which is about 25 minutes away from Sorsogon City.
